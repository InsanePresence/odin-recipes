Some Pizza

Hmm pizza yummy. This page will teach you how to make bread with cheese n shit on it. I fucking love cheese on bread with red sauce. Mmmm.

Ingredients

  • Large, circle cheese
  • Bread
  • Slimy red sauce, source unknown
  • 'Ronis
  • Garlic powder for the puffy edge bread

How to make the yummy bread

  1. Go into debt buying real cheese.
  2. Take out a second mortgage to afford bread.
  3. Make expensive cheese into circle through any means necessary.
  4. Pray to the bread god to turn your bread loaf into a flat circle slightly larger than the cheese circle.
  5. Slather the unknown red paste all over that bitch.
  6. Carefully place cheese circle on top of that shit.
  7. Write a random placement algorithm from scratch in py to load into your 'roni placement device.
  8. Use 'roni placement device to scatter 'ronis all over the circle cheese.
  9. Bake for some time, guess I dare you.
  10. Consume pizza and smile big.
